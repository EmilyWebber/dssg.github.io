FAQs for Applicants interested in becoming Summer Fellows

Apply to be a Summer Fellow

How do I apply?

Fill out the application form by February 1, 2013 and make sure your references also fill out the recommendation form

When does the fellowship start and finish?

We'll start end of May and finish end of August. We're working on figuring out multiple start dates that can accomodate different end of quarter/semester dates

Is it a full-time program and do I have to be in Chicago?

Yes. And Yes.

Do I have to be a student to apply?

Not neccesarily but this is designed for current student or recent graduates. If you have finished a degree (undergrad, Masters, Phd) in the past couple of years, you should be ok to apply.
