2015 Projects

During the program, our fellows work on data science projects in healthcare, education, energy, transportation, and more.

Our program is intensive and hands-on. Fellows work with nonprofits, local governments, and federal agencies, learning about what they do and tackling data problems they face.

We believe in open knowledge and open source. We blog extensively about our projects, and make much of our research and code open to everyone.
